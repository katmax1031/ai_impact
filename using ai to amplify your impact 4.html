Introduction

As researchers, scientists, and developers, R&D employees are uniquely positioned to amplify their impact and their career by adopting AI tools.

We are naturally curious, but skeptical.

Common AI
Misconceptions

Myth:
AI will take my job

Reality: R&D is very context driven, nonstandard, and nonlinear. How often do you do the exact same task? AI will transform what type of work is valuable but cannot replace your critical thinking & judgment.

🤖

Myth:
AI understands what it writes and creates

Reality: LLMs do not comprehend meaning and do not think. Imagine they are more like a hyper-advanced version of your phone's predictive text. LLMs predict patterns but do not truly "reason" (that would be AGI).

📱

Myth:
AI will make me dumber

Reality: While there certainly is a trap to avoid of "cognitive offloading", engaging AI in conversation loops where you remain the final editor and curator becomes merely a toolkit to reach your goals faster.

🤦‍♂️

Myth:
AI is always right

Reality: While AI has vast sets of training data, it can and will hallucinate inaccurate information. It has no ground source of what is "truth", it only sees patterns. Human judgement for safe and responsible use of AI remains critical.
